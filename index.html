What is NAVKRITI?

The term NAVKRITI is a Sanskrit word. In sanskrit "NAVA" means "New" and "KRITI" means "Creation" or "Composition". So "Navkriti" could be translated to "New Creation" or "New Composition" Navkriti is the annual technnical fest of Chaibasa Engineering college. Welcome to the Navkriti, the ultimate destination for technology enthusiasts, innovations and visionaries!
